Various Types of Google Ads Campaigns

Before investing in Google Ads, it’s essential to thoroughly understand the different types of campaigns available, how each works, and key Google Ads terminology. Here are five main types of Google Ad campaigns that you can choose from, depending on your goals. Each is designed for a specific purpose, as outlined below:

Shopping Ads:

If you're looking to promote a specific product or a collection, Shopping Ads are an excellent choice. By partnering with a certified Google display ads agency, you can provide key details about your products, which Google then uses to create the shopping campaign. These ads include images, product descriptions, pricing, and other relevant information, making it easier for potential customers to browse and buy. You can also adjust or remove the details as needed.

Video Ads:

For brands seeking high conversion rates and enhanced visibility, video ads are the way to go. YouTube, as a popular search engine, offers a powerful platform for video ad campaigns. You’ve probably encountered these ads while watching or searching for videos on YouTube. Video ads help increase brand awareness and customer engagement by placing your promotional content right in front of your target audience during their search or viewing sessions.

App Ads:

If you're looking to promote a mobile app, Google’s App Ads are the ideal solution. Whether for Android or iOS, App Ads allow you to showcase your app without having to design a complex campaign. Simply provide the app’s details and your target audience information, and Google will handle the rest. App ads can encourage users to download your app or prompt existing users to take specific actions within the app.

Search Ads:

Search Ads are an excellent way to reach your audience through text-based ads that appear in Google search results. Certified Google Search Ads partners in Hyderabad can help place your brand's promotional ads at the top of search results, making it easier for users to discover your products or services while searching for related information. These ads look like regular search results, making them less intrusive and more likely to attract clicks.

Display Ads:

When it comes to capturing attention, Display Ads are highly effective. These ads use eye-catching images to attract users to your brand or product. Display ad campaigns typically operate on a pay-per-click or pay-per-impression basis, meaning you only pay when users interact with your ads. With visually appealing graphics, display ads are a powerful way to showcase your brand and engage potential customers.
